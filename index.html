Understanding Your Blood Test

Blood tests are a common medical procedure that helps doctors check for a wide range of conditions, assess organ function, and monitor treatment effectiveness. Common blood tests include a Complete Blood Count (CBC), Cholesterol Panel, and Glucose Test.

Common Blood Test Abbreviations:

  • CBC: Complete Blood Count
  • LDL/HDL: Low/High-Density Lipoprotein (Cholesterol)
  • HbA1c: Hemoglobin A1c (Blood Sugar)
